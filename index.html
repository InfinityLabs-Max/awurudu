Observing the New Moon

March 30 (Sun) & May 1 (Thu)

The new moon is observed as an important transition in the Sinhala New Year calendar.

පරණ අවුරුද්ද සඳහා ස්නානය

Ritual Bath for the Old Year

April 13 (Sun)

Take a herbal bath using Divul leaves juice before the old year ends.

අලුත් අවුරුදු උදාව

Dawn of the New Year

April 14 (Mon) 3:21 AM

Official transition from the old year to the new. Light lamps and begin rituals.

පුණ්ය කාලය

Neutral Period

April 13, 8:57 PM to April 14, 9:45 AM

All work paused for religious activities. First meal prepared during auspicious time.

ආහර පිසීම

First Meal Preparation

April 14, 4:04 AM

Wear copper-colored clothes, face south, prepare kiribath and sweetmeats.

වැඩ ඇල්ලීම

Starting Work & Transactions

April 14, 6:44 AM

Wear white clothes, face south, resume work and share first meal.

හිසතෙල් ගෑම

Anointing of Oil

April 16, 9:04 AM

Wear green clothes, face north, apply herbal oil with kohomba leaves.

රැකීරක්ෂා සඳහා පිටත්ව යෑම

Stepping Out for Prosperity

April 17, 9:03 AM

Wear gold clothes, eat kiribath, face north to begin new journeys.
